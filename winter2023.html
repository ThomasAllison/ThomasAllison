Greetings from Seattle!

Winter 2023

The winter has officially come and gone. And this is the first season I lived in Seattle! I moved here without knowing what I was getting into. My cousin, Ronan, told me I'd love the city. Turns out, he's right! So many new things to discover. Not did I move to a new city, but also started my new job in January.

I took the picture in the banner above one week before my first day. Watch the YouTube short on the race to make it to Kerry Park before the sunset was over. I got very lucky with Mount Rainier in the background. I don't think I'll ever get tired from all the beautiful mountains that surround the city.

I moved into an apartment in Capitol Hill. Close to Anderson Park and the Lightrail station. Capitol Hill is packed with bars, coffee shops and gay clubs. I already enjoyed this place in the winter, but on beautiful days the neighborhood would come alive. People outside in the park walking their dogs, playing all kinds of sports and hanging out.

And I'm also proud to announce that I now own my first car! I'm so excited to go out and explore all of Washington with it. The first real trip I took it on was with Ben to Shi Shi beach on the North Western part of the Olympic Peninsula. We got so lucky with the weather. Mostly clear skies and sun. But after the sun set over the Pasific, it got cold. A good reminder that it was still January.
